Margarita

Description:

One of the most famous cocktails with a great taste, with his fancy cocktail glass, Margaritas can be served either shaken with ice, without ice, or blended with ice.

Ingredients:

  • Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Triple sec
  • Tequila

How to make it:

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass
  3. Add snowflake ices on it
